About U CAN FIRE

I learned the basics of money from a short pamphlet and a few clear books — simple ideas that changed how I think about work, saving and investing. From there I focused on the essentials: how behaviour matters, why low-cost index funds work, and how to build a stable financial foundation.

Why I built this

Financial advice in Canada is scattered and often noisy. I built U CAN FIRE to give a direct, judgment-free place with clear steps people can follow — no ads, no upsells, just practical guidance.

What the name means

U — you (and a nod to learning).
CAN — Canada (and a reminder you can learn this).
FIRE — Financial Independence, Retire Early: not necessarily quitting work, but having the freedom to choose.

A different mindset

This project encourages a quieter approach: focus on your goals, spend intentionally, and ignore comparison. Financial independence isn’t about deprivation — it’s about building options that suit your life.

How this helps

This site isn’t a substitute for professional advice. It’s a place to learn the basics, build confidence, and take the next practical step toward financial resilience. If learning these ideas helped me, I want others to have the same head start.
