Stage 6 — Long-Term Freedom (FI Basics & Planning)

When you’ve built stability, eliminated debt, saved a buffer, and started investing, you’re ready for long-term planning. This stage focuses on refining your goals — whether that’s Coast FI, early retirement, traditional retirement, or simply having more flexibility.

Here, you’ll look at your savings rate, investment allocation, long-term projections, and lifestyle choices. You’ll start asking questions like: “How much is enough?” “What do I want the next decades of my life to look like?” and “How do I balance enjoying today with preparing for tomorrow?”

This stage isn’t about perfection. It’s about intentionality — aligning your money with the life you want, not just the life you happen to fall into.

Checklist

  • Define long-term goals (FI, Coast FI, retirement)
  • Understand your savings rate
  • Review investment allocation annually
  • Balance spending, saving, and enjoyment
