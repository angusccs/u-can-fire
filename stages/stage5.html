Stage 5 — Start Investing (Index Funds & ETFs)

Once your foundation is stable, you can begin investing for long-term growth. Simple, low-fee index funds or ETFs are the most reliable way for most Canadians to build wealth over time. They’re diversified, low cost, and require almost no maintenance.

This stage is about getting comfortable with the basics: choosing a brokerage, automating contributions, understanding risk tolerance, and avoiding speculation or stock-picking. You don’t need to be an expert — just consistent and patient.

The key is to invest regularly, ignore short-term market noise, and let compounding do its work. A simple long-term strategy beats complicated short-term tactics almost every time.

Checklist

  • Open a brokerage account (Wealthsimple, Questrade, etc.)
  • Choose a simple ETF (e.g., all-in-one or broad-market)
  • Set up monthly/bi-weekly contributions
  • Ignore predictions and focus long-term
